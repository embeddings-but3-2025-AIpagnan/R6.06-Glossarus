# Library Glossary
### This glossary brings together essential terms used in the field of libraries and document management.

| Word | Definition | Synonyms |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Book | A set of written or printed sheets bound together | Tome, Volume, Publication |
| Borrow | To take and use something temporarily before returning it | Check out, Loan |
| Periodical | A publication issued at regular intervals | Magazine, Journal, Serial |
| Shelf | A flat surface used for storing or displaying items | Rack, Ledge |
| Film | A sequence of moving images, often with sound, that tells a story, documents an event, or presents an artistic idea | Movie, Motion Picture, Flick |
| Catalog | A complete list of items, typically in systematic order | Inventory, List, Directory |
